01 — About Me

The designer who
also codes.

I'm a Computer Science student with a genuine passion for design. While my peers dive deep into algorithms and data structures, I find myself drawn to the intersection of technology and human experience — the part where code becomes something people actually feel.

In every group project, I naturally gravitate toward the design work. Whether it's mocking up a UI, refining visual hierarchies, or pushing pixels until a layout feels just right — it's where I come alive.

I believe good design isn't decoration. It's architecture. It shapes behavior, builds trust, and makes complex systems feel effortless.
